Jesse Hoekstra

I am a second-year DPhil in Statistics student at the Univesity of Oxford, Nuffield College, and recipient of the Clarendon Fund Scholarship. I am advised by Frank Windmeijer and Yee Whye Teh. My work encompasses the intersection of statistics, econometrics, and machine learning. In particular, I have a strong interest in causality, the intricacies of deep learning methodologies, and their interplay in understanding complex systems.

Before starting my PhD, I graduated with distinction and cum laude from the London School of Economics and Political Science (MSc) and the University of Amsterdam (BSc), focusing on econometrical and machine learning methodologies.
